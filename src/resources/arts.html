Career Options in Arts

Journalism and Mass Communication

This field includes careers in print journalism, broadcast journalism, public relations, advertising, and digital media. You can work as a journalist, editor, content creator, or media strategist.

Psychology

A degree in psychology can lead to careers in counseling, clinical psychology, industrial-organizational psychology, or research in psychological science.

Law

If you're interested in the legal profession, you can pursue a career in law by completing a Bachelor's in Law (LLB) degree and subsequently passing the bar exam.

Public Administration

Public administration professionals work in government and public-sector organizations, managing policies, budgets, and public services.

Archaeology and Anthropology

These fields involve the study of human societies and cultures, both past and present. Career opportunities include research, academia, and cultural resource management.

Travel and Tourism

Careers in this field include tour guiding, travel writing, tourism management, and hospitality.

Fashion Design and Merchandising

Fashion designers create clothing and accessories, while merchandisers manage the marketing and sales of fashion products.

Writing and Publishing

If you have strong writing skills, you can pursue a career in writing, journalism, editing, or publishing.
