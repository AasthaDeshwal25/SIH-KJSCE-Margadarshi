Career Options in Vocational Courses

Diploma in Engineering

Pursuing a diploma in engineering can lead to careers as technicians or supervisors in various engineering fields such as electrical, mechanical, civil, and electronics.

ITI (Industrial Training Institute) Courses

ITI courses provide training in technical and trade skills like electrician, plumber, welder, mechanic, and carpenter, leading to careers as skilled tradespeople.

Hotel Management

Vocational courses in hotel management can prepare you for careers in the hospitality industry as chefs, hotel managers, or front desk staff in hotels, resorts, and restaurants.

Healthcare and Allied Health Sciences

Completing vocational courses in healthcare fields like nursing, medical laboratory technology, and radiology technology can lead to roles in healthcare facilities.

Automotive Technology

Vocational programs related to automotive technology can prepare you for careers as automotive technicians, mechanics, or service advisors in the automotive industry.
